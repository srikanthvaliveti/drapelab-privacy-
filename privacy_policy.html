Overview

DrapeLab ("we", "our", or "us") is a fashion design application built for designers and creators. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using DrapeLab, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Design data — motifs, patterns, sketches, and garment configurations you create and save within the app.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as feature usage frequency, crash reports, and app performance data to help us improve the app.
  • Images you upload — reference images or garment photos you choose to upload for features such as Style Analyser or background reference. These are processed and stored securely.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve DrapeLab's features.
  • To process AI-powered features (motif generation, style analysis, AI render) by sending prompts or images to third-party AI providers on your behalf.
  • To manage your account, subscription, and credit balance.
  • To send you important service-related communications (e.g. password reset, billing).
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

DrapeLab uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Data is stored on Supabase-managed servers. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — AI image generation and text analysis (DALL-E 3, GPT-4o). Prompts and images may be sent to OpenAI's API. Privacy Policy
  • OpenRouter — AI model routing for Style Analyser feature. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play — app distribution and in-app subscription billing. Privacy Policy

Data Storage & Security

Your account data and designs are stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

AI-generated images are downloaded to your device's temporary storage and are not permanently stored on our servers beyond what is needed to complete the request.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and saved designs for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and designs are permanently deleted within 30 days.

Crash logs and anonymous usage data may be retained for up to 12 months for diagnostic purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your design data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

DrapeLab is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
